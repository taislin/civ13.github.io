# Humanity’s Timeline is Yours to Forge.

![Civ13 Banner](public/img/banner.png)

**Civilization 13** is a high-fidelity sandbox survival and tactical combat game built on the deep, interactive mechanics of the Space Station 13 engine. From the dawn of the first tribe to the industrial machinery of the modern age, Civ13 lets you experience—and influence—every era of human history.

---

## 🏔️ Nomads Mode: From Zero to Civilization
The core Civ13 experience. You spawn with nothing but a flint knife, basic rags, and your wits. In a world with no pre-determined borders, you choose your path:

*   **Scavenge & Survive:** Forage for resources, hunt wildlife, and brave the elements.
*   **Build Your Faction:** Form tribes with other players to claim territory. 
*   **Innovate:** Collect Research Points to drag your society out of the Stone Age. Watch as your crude wooden huts evolve into stone fortresses and eventually brick factories.
*   **Diplomacy or War:** Trade with neighboring civilizations or raise an army to seize their technology.

![Nomads Gameplay](public/img/nomads.png)

---

## ⚔️ Tactical Combat: The Clash of Eras
For those who crave the front lines, our **Team-Deathmatch (TDM)** and **Combat** modes offer meticulously researched historical scenarios. Master the unique tactics of every century:

*   **Ancient Warfare:** Shields, spears, and formation-based melee.
*   **The Age of Gunpowder:** Volley fire, slow-loading muskets, and heavy cannons.
*   **Modern Conflict:** Trench warfare, armored vehicles, and tactical firearms.
